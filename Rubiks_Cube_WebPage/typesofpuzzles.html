Types of Puzzles

Ever since the world of cubing blew up, there has been many types of puzzles based off of the cube itself. First, there came higher order versions of the rubiks cube. Examples include the 4x4 and 5x5. There were also some lower order versions like the 2x2 and even the 1x1.

Shapemods were also slowly popularizing. Shape mods were originally made by individuals who somehow modified the cube to move, look, and solve differently from the normal cube.
